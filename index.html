Pure Industrial is one of Canada's leading providers of industrial real estate. The company aims to transform the Canadian warehousing and distribution landscape by offering a best-in-class industrial real estate portfolio and full-service supply chain solutions for customers, from the first to the last mile. Pure Industrial operates in key regions across Canada, including the Greater Toronto Area, Greater Vancouver Area, Greater Montreal Area, Quebec City, and other parts of Canada.

Pure Industrial's portfolio includes millions of square feet of industrial space, strategically located to support the Canadian supply chain. The company is committed to sustainability and responsible business practices, as highlighted in its inaugural Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Report. This report underscores Pure Industrial's dedication to sustainable growth and its strategic vision for the future.
