My Role at Pure Industrial

Initially, my role as the Energy and Sustainability Analyst Intern focused on the collection and analysis of energy and sustainability data. I assisted in mapping out new sustainability programs and identifying opportunities for energy savings and incentives across capital and operational projects. This gave me the chance to make a meaningful contribution to the company’s sustainability efforts while understanding the impact of energy management on business operations.

As the term progressed, however, my responsibilities evolved towards revamping Pure Industrial's Workiva workspace. This shift required a greater focus on the company’s internal systems, including enhancing workflows, automating processes, and integrating datasets. In this regard, MySQL and Python became essential tools to address various technical challenges. These tasks demanded a thorough understanding of database management and automation, areas I had honed both in class and through prior hands-on experiences.

This role required a combination of technical and collaborative skills. I applied my proficiency in MySQL, Python, and data automation to streamline processes and enhance data workflows. In addition, working with cross-functional teams, including Data Analytics and Property Directors helped me improve my communication and problem-solving skills. I gained valuable insights into how sustainability initiatives are intertwined with data management, real estate, and business operations.

The most interesting aspect of my role was the opportunity to blend technical problem-solving with sustainability goals. While my primary focus initially was on energy data, the pivot to Workiva optimization provided me with an opportunity to directly impact organizational efficiency by improving data integration and automation. This experience deepened my technical skills while giving me a better understanding of how technology can help drive sustainability in business.
